Exercise & Techniques

The Advanced Cobra

By providing a conscious opening in the chest and stretch in the shoulders, Cobra Pose, called Bhujangasana in Sanskrit, fights fatigue and relieves lower back pain, boosting both the energetic and the physical body.

The Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II)

This routine enhances flexibility in your hips, groin, and shoulders while expanding your chest and improving lung capacity. It boosts endurance and focus, revitalizes weary muscles, and activates your abdominal organs. Particularly beneficial during the second trimester, it alleviates back pain. Additionally, it fosters equilibrium, strengthens stability, and enhances blood flow and breathing.
